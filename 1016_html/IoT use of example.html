IoT use of example

Smart home


With the development and popularization of the Internet, every household has the Internet, and the demand for smart homes is increasing. Users can use smart home apps to send commands over the Internet to control the Internet-connected smart homes at home. For example, an app can switch smart light bulbs on and off. Intelligent fan, users can use the app to control the fan's airspeed and automatic power on and off. The sweeping robot allows users to set the robot to clean up the garbage in the home regularly and operate it without having to use it at home, automating all processes.

Transportation system


IoT can help traffic networks command and share real-time traffic conditions. Through the map app, users can view the road surface of traffic in real-time to avoid busy traffic sections. Traffic lights can connect to the Internet through nearby signal towers, report relevant road conditions, and make corresponding judgments, so that traffic can flow, reducing safety risks and saving drivers time in traffic jams. Logistics companies can report the truck's transportation progress to buyers through IoT, and the truck driver and company can also remotely monitor the status of the goods in the car through the network.

Teletherapy for remote areas or third-world countries, doctors can conduct remote therapy online, reduce local medical expenses, help reduce close contact, avoid spreading the virus, and ensure medical combat strength.
