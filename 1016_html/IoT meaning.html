What is IoT ?

IoT (Internet of Things) is a concept that connects everything to the Internet, including transportation, home devices, and even clothes. This technology uses the internet to transfer data over the internet; it allows them to automate tasks.

IoT systems include sensors, networks, and software, allowing them to collect data from users and the environment. This data automates tasks like recessed lights, fans, etc.

IoT also provides tons of applications, like healthcare, smart home, smart cities, industrial, and transportation; it is a technology that can affect our daily life in the future.
